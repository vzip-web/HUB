VZiP's operations are divided among three primary pillars. The documentation formats (invoices, proposals) must be flexible enough to accommodate the distinct billing structures and project milestones of each division.

  • ■

    Software Development (45%)

    Web Development (35%)

  • ■

    Business Consulting (20%)

    Hourly tracking and strategic agreements needing strict legal formatting.
